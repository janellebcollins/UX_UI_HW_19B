About

Janelle Collins is UX Designer based in Charlotte, NC who creates clean and unique designs for an intuitive and enjoyable UX.

She gained her love for design as freelance graphic designer. Janelle enjoys creating designs for small businesses because she is able to help entrepreneurs discover innovative ways to present their products and services to their target audiences.

In her freetime, Janelle loves to curate playlists for her friends and followers to jam out to.

Whether she is prototyping a new mobile app, designing marketing materials, or developing a Summer Playlist, Janelle always seeks to provide exciting experiences that the users value!
